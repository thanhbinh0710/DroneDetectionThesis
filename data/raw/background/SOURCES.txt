# FREE BACKGROUND AUDIO SOURCES

## 1. Freesound.org
Website: https://freesound.org/
- Search terms: "traffic", "wind", "rain", "city", "nature", "birds", "machinery"
- Filter by: License (Creative Commons), Format (WAV), Duration (5-30s)
- Examples:
  * City traffic: https://freesound.org/search/?q=city+traffic&f=type:wav
  * Wind noise: https://freesound.org/search/?q=wind+noise&f=type:wav
  * Birds: https://freesound.org/search/?q=birds+chirping&f=type:wav

## 2. BBC Sound Effects
Website: https://sound-effects.bbcrewind.co.uk/
- Free for research and education
- Categories: Nature, Transport, Industry, Weather
- High quality professional recordings

## 3. ESC-50 Dataset (Environmental Sound Classification)
GitHub: https://github.com/karolpiczak/ESC-50
- 50 classes of environmental sounds
- 2000 audio clips (40 per class)
- Useful classes:
  * Dog barking
  * Rain
  * Wind
  * Airplane
  * Helicopter
  * Car horn
  * Engine

## 4. UrbanSound8K Dataset
Website: https://urbansounddataset.weill.cornell.edu/urbansound8k.html
- 8732 labeled sound excerpts (<=4s)
- 10 classes: air_conditioner, car_horn, children_playing, dog_bark, drilling, 
              engine_idling, gun_shot, jackhammer, siren, street_music

## 5. AudioSet (Google)
Website: https://research.google.com/audioset/
- Ontology of 632 audio event classes
- Can download specific classes via YouTube IDs

## 6. YouTube Audio Library
Website: https://www.youtube.com/audiolibrary
- Free royalty-free music and sound effects
- Filter by category: Sound Effects → Ambience, Nature, Urban

## 7. Record Your Own
Use a microphone or smartphone to record:
- ✓ Office ambient noise
- ✓ Traffic from your window
- ✓ Park/outdoor sounds
- ✓ Fan, air conditioner
- ✓ Rain, wind
- ✓ Birds in your area

## Recommended Download Strategy:
1. Download 10 samples from 3 different sources (30 total)
2. Convert to WAV if needed: `ffmpeg -i input.mp3 output.wav`
3. Resample to 44100 Hz: `ffmpeg -i input.wav -ar 44100 output.wav`
4. Rename to BACKGROUND_001.wav, BACKGROUND_002.wav, etc.
5. Run: `python scripts/add_background_samples.py`

## Quality Checklist:
- [ ] Format: WAV (not MP3, not FLAC)
- [ ] Sample Rate: 44100 Hz
- [ ] Duration: 5-30 seconds
- [ ] Volume: Normalized (not too quiet or too loud)
- [ ] Variety: Different types of non-drone sounds
- [ ] No copyright issues (CC license or public domain)
